Dear froshies,

Even though I can't write, I just wanted to pop in and say a few words to you all. Enjoy these few years in uni, make the most out of it. So here's a list of my top few tips

Talk to new people. talk to your profs, talk to your ta. Everyone has such different experiences and you'll never know about it until you say hi. Open those doors

Grades aren't everything, I was in the bottom 20% of my year but I think I turned out great. Focus more on understanding the topics and material than studying for the test. it makes life easier

Find your passion, and if you don't just find what you dislike. Join clubs, try classes, figure out what you want to do ! and if you can't decide, at least figure out what you don't want to do.

Use your first 2-3 years to explore clubs and associations, and your last 2 to focus on making an impact on one. It's never too late

You'll learn more from mistakes than playing it safe. So make mistakes basically

And finally relax and enjoy uni, you won't get to do it again once you graduate and by then you still won't learn how to spell.

Again, Relax,

Ali Seifeldin
