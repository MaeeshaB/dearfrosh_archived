Dear Frosh is a series of letters collected from U of T Engineering students addressed to their first year selves.
This initiative is created and facilitated by the 1% Inspiration Podcast, a U of T engineering based podcast about making the most of an engineering (and undergraduate) education. It is inspired by the original "Dear Penn Freshmen" launched at the University of Pennsylvania in 2015 by Lauren McCann.
