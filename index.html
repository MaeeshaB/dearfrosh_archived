Dear Frosh is a series of messages collected from U of T Engineering students addressed to their first year selves and incoming engineering students.
This initiative was originally founded and facilitated by the 1% Inspiration Podcast, a U of T engineering based podcast about making the most of an engineering (and undergraduate) education. In 2023, we collaborated with the Troost Institute for Leadership Education in Engineering to deliver Dear 2T7 postcards. We welcome you to explore them them below.
